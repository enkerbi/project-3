Objective feelings. The property of things. When the property of something is loveable the emotion of envy comes. When the other is the object of hatred since the property of the other is hateful the emotion of ease occurs.

My heart
Consoles the friend
Who has failed
The higher civil service examination--
Is that not taking pleasure?

My friend,
Who lives peacefully
On a pension,
Passes by, looking at the field
Where I plant rice.
